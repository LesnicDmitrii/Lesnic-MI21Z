What is Space Odyssey?

A Space Odyssey is a free web collection of basic facts about astronomy and cosmology; new images of the nearest corners of our Universe; and simply an educational guide for space lovers

Space Odyssey gives you the opportunity to explore our planet, solar system, galaxy and universe. Provided free of charge, Space Odyssey is developed by a team of educators and communications specialists who collaborate to ensure content is accurate, relevant, engaging, relevant and accessible to a wide audience.
